About PillVault

We believe that medication management should be simple, private, and accessible to everyone — regardless of age, ability, or budget.

Our Mission

Millions of people around the world take daily medications. Yet studies show that nearly 50% of patients don't take their medications as prescribed — leading to preventable hospitalizations, health complications, and increased healthcare costs.

PillVault was created to solve this problem with a simple, thoughtful approach. We built an app that makes it effortless to organize medications, set reliable reminders, and track inventory — all without compromising on privacy or charging a single cent.

Our mission is to help every person — from busy professionals managing supplements to seniors taking multiple prescriptions — stay on top of their medication schedule with confidence and ease.

Our Values

Every decision we make is guided by these core principles:

🔒

Privacy First

Your health data is deeply personal. PillVault stores everything locally on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking — ever.

♿

Accessibility

We design for everyone. WCAG AAA contrast ratios, large touch targets, screen reader support, and clear typography make PillVault usable by all.

🎯

Technology should simplify your life, not complicate it. PillVault focuses on what matters — helping you take your pills on time, nothing more.

💚

What Makes PillVault Different

The app store is filled with medication reminder apps, but most of them share common problems: they require accounts, collect your data, show ads, or lock essential features behind paywalls. PillVault takes a fundamentally different approach.

No Account Required. Download and start using PillVault immediately. No sign-up, no email verification, no passwords. Your medication data doesn't need to be tied to an identity.

Truly Offline. PillVault works without an internet connection. All reminders, inventory tracking, and data management happen entirely on your device. Perfect for travel, areas with limited connectivity, or anyone who values independence from cloud services.

Smart Inventory Management. Beyond simple reminders, PillVault actively tracks your pill supply. It calculates remaining days of medication, shows visual progress bars, and sends low-stock alerts before you run out — so refills are never a surprise.

Thoughtful Design. Built with Material Design 3 principles, PillVault offers a clean, modern interface with light and dark themes. Every color, spacing, and interaction has been carefully crafted for clarity and usability.

Customizable to Your Needs. From snooze durations to notification sounds, time formats to reorder thresholds — PillVault adapts to how you want to manage your medications, not the other way around.

Built with Modern Technology

PillVault is built using industry-leading technologies to ensure performance, reliability, and a great user experience:

  • React Native & Expo — Cross-platform framework for fast, smooth mobile performance
  • TypeScript — Type-safe code for reliability and fewer bugs
  • Material Design 3 — Google's latest design language for a familiar, modern interface
  • Expo Notifications — Reliable, precise notification delivery system
  • Local-first architecture — All data stays on your device with zero server dependency
