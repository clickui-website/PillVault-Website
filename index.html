FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PillVault really free?

Yes, PillVault is 100% free with no in-app purchases, no subscriptions, and no hidden fees. All features are available to every user from day one.

Does PillVault collect my health data?

No. PillVault stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal or health information on external servers. Your data stays on your phone.

Do I need an account to use PillVault?

No account or sign-up is required. Simply download the app and start adding your medications right away. No email, no password, no verification needed.

Can I set different reminder times for different days?

Yes! PillVault supports fully customizable reminder schedules. You can set different times for each day of the week, or use preset frequencies like once, twice, or three times daily.

What happens if I miss a dose?

PillVault automatically detects missed doses after 60 minutes and marks them accordingly in your history. You can also snooze reminders to be notified again after a configurable delay.

How does inventory tracking work?

When you add a medication, enter your current quantity. Each time you mark a dose as taken, PillVault automatically decreases your inventory count. You'll see remaining days of supply and get alerts when stock runs low.

Can I export my medication data?

Yes. Go to Settings and use the Export Data feature. PillVault exports all your pills, history, and settings as a portable JSON file that you can save or share.

Does PillVault work without internet?

Absolutely. PillVault works completely offline. All reminder scheduling, inventory tracking, and data management happen locally on your device with no internet connection required.

Is PillVault available for iPhone or iOS?

PillVault is currently available on Android via the Google Play Store. iOS support is planned for a future release. Stay tuned for updates!

How do I contact support?

Visit our Support page for help. You can reach us via email for any questions, feedback, or issues you encounter with the app.
